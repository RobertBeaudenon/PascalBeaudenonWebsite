About Pascal Beaudenon

Pascal Beaudenon is a landscape photographer specializing in panoramic photography of Lebanon's diverse natural environments. With years of exploration across the country, from the coastal regions of Jbeil to the mountain peaks of the Anti-Lebanon Range, Pascal captures the essence of Lebanon's natural beauty.

His work documents the changing seasons, the play of light across landscapes, and the untold stories of Lebanon's natural heritage. Through careful composition and technical excellence, each photograph reveals the hidden beauty of familiar and remote locations alike.
