About Beiteddine Festival

The Beiteddine Art Festival is one of Lebanon's most prestigious annual cultural events, held at the historic Beiteddine Palace in the Chouf Mountains. Since its inception, the festival has showcased the finest in music, dance, theater, and visual arts, attracting international audiences and renowned artists.

Pascal Beaudenon's exhibition of panoramic Lebanese landscapes was featured as part of the festival's visual arts program, bringing attention to Lebanon's natural beauty through his distinctive wide-format photography technique.
